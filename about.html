Amber Imani Roberts

Hi! My name is Amber and I am your next Interior Designer. I come from a professional background in Project Management, but have always had a creative side that ran wild. About 9 years ago at the age of 25, I finally discovered my passion for Interior Design & Organization when I decorated my first big girl apartment. From there I could not stop organizing and helping out my friends in their spaces, and I knew this was what I needed to pursue. I have completed many projects of different shapes & sizes, and along with quality I pride myself on being affordable for any budget.
